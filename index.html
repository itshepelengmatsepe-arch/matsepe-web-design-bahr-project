About us

(Tovah) ; meaning excellence in the Hebrew language was established by a group of architecture graduates from the Limkokwing University of Creative Technology in the year 2000, one of the co-founders Mary discovered,the lack of creativity in house designs in both the suburban and township areas.

With the lack of originality and increase in cookie-cutter designs , she saw an opportunity to bring dreams into reality as our mission says “style should not be limited”, we offer creative designs that fit your budget and meet your creativity expectations.
